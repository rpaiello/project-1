Levels for Portal 2

One of my lifelong hobbies has been creating puzzles for Portal 2.

While I was in college, I even assembled a 9-map campaign of puzzles. They are quite complex and use modded testing elements. While not perfect by any means, I like to think the campaign holds a certain standard of quality.

"Fullbright Haunted Houses"

Capstone project of a Graphic Design class I took. The original assignment was to make a booklet or pamphlet on whatever subject we wanted, the requirements being that we demonstrate good graphic design and layout principles; as well as a cohesive and consistent design language. After roughly a month of writing and screenshot-ting I emerged bleary-eyed with a 60 page book extolling the artistic merit of my favorite Garry's Mod maps.

You can read/download it here!
